Why Hire an 18-Wheeler Accident Lawyer

Being part of an 18-wheeler accident often leads to trauma: both physical and emotional. The other vehicle is often damaged beyond repair, and the occupants often incur serious and sometimes fatal injuries.

If you or your loved one has been hit by an 18-wheeler, you can and should hire an attorney. Experienced 18-wheeler injury lawyers can help you receive compensation for:

  • Past and future medical treatment
  • Property damage and replacement
  • Physical disabilities or death due to the incident
  • Lost wages from missing work
  • Pain and suffering for you or your family

The Danger of 18-Wheelers

Accidents involving 18-wheelers often cause serious injuries for the driver of the other vehicle, along with any pedestrians. Federal laws are in place to prevent these accidents, but thousands still occur every year. These incidents can cause:

  • Traumatic brain injuries
  • Spinal cord or neck injuries
  • Disfigurement or amputations

Most truck injury cases end in a settlement between the at-fault party and victim. If your 18-wheeler accident lawyer has proof that you were the victim of a careless act, then the responsible party will prefer to settle rather than risk a massive verdict.

Reaching a settlement benefits you, the victim, in numerous ways:

  • Large recoveries, up to millions of dollars
  • Quick compensation to offset medical bills and lost wages
  • Maintaining your privacy
